<p><p>The collagen injections market is primarily segmented into bovine collagen and human-based collagen types. Bovine collagen, derived from cow sources, is commonly used for cosmetic procedures and support in skin elasticity, though it carries a risk of allergic reactions. Human-based collagen, sourced from donor tissues, is preferred for its biocompatibility and reduced rejection rates, often offering enhanced results in aesthetics and therapeutic applications. Both types cater to diverse consumer preferences, driving growth in the cosmetic and medical industries.</p></p>
