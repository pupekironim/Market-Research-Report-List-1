<p><p>The commercial printing market comprises several techniques catering to various needs. **Offset printing** is widely used for high-volume projects, delivering sharp images through a process adjusting ink on plates. **Intaglio printing** involves incised designs, producing high-quality results often seen in banknotes and fine art. **Digital printing** offers flexibility and quick turnaround for short runs, utilizing digital files to create prints without plates. Other methods include flexography for packaging and screen printing for textiles, each serving distinct market demands.</p></p>
