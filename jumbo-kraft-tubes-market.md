<p><p>Jumbo Kraft tubes are categorized into single ply, double ply, and triple ply based on their construction and strength. Single ply tubes consist of one layer of kraft paper, offering basic durability for lightweight applications. Double ply tubes feature two layers, providing enhanced strength and resistance, suitable for medium-weight loads. Triple ply tubes, with three layers, offer maximum strength and durability for heavy-duty applications, making them ideal for packaging and shipping heavier products. Each type serves specific needs across various industries.</p></p>
