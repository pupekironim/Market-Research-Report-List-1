<p><p>The card reader-writer market is divided into two main types: wireless and wired. Wireless card readers utilize technologies like Bluetooth or NFC, enabling seamless transactions without physical connections, which enhances mobility and ease of use. They are ideal for on-the-go applications. In contrast, wired card readers connect directly through USB or similar interfaces, providing stable, uninterrupted performance and often faster data transfer. These are commonly used in retail environments where secure, constant connections are prioritized for processing transactions.</p></p>
