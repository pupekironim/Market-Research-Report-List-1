<p><p>Inverting regulators are power supply circuits that convert a positive input voltage to a negative output voltage. The market for inverting regulators can be divided into two main types: integrated switch and external switch. Integrated switch regulators have all components integrated into a single chip, offering compact designs and ease of use, making them ideal for consumer electronics. External switch regulators, on the other hand, employ discrete components for greater flexibility and performance, commonly used in industrial applications where higher power and customization are required.</p></p>
