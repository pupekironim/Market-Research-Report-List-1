<p><p>The flexible packaging lamination adhesives market encompasses three main types: solvent-based adhesives, solvent-free adhesives, and water-based adhesives. Solvent-based adhesives comprise organic solvents, providing excellent adhesion and clarity but raising environmental concerns. Solvent-free adhesives contain no solvents, ensuring safer applications with reduced environmental impact, making them increasingly popular. Water-based adhesives use water as a carrier, offering lower volatile organic compound (VOC) emissions and enhanced sustainability without compromising performance, thus appealing to environmentally conscious manufacturers and consumers.</p></p>
