Roller conveyors utilize a series of rollers to move items, offering low-friction transfer ideal for heavy loads and efficient sorting. Belt conveyors, on the other hand, use a continuous belt to transport goods, providing versatility for various materials and sizes. Both types support automation in manufacturing and logistics, enhancing efficiency by streamlining the movement of products within facilities.</p></p>
