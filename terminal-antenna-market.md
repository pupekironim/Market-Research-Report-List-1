Base station antennas are used in telecommunications networks to facilitate communication between the base station and user devices, primarily focusing on coverage and capacity. Terminal antennas, on the other hand, are installed on user devices like smartphones, routers, and IoT devices, enabling them to connect to the network. Both types play crucial roles in ensuring efficient data transmission and connectivity in various applications.</p></p>
