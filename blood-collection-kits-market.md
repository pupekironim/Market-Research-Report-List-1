<p><ul><li>Venous Blood Collection Kits</li><li>Capillary Blood Collection Kits</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The blood collection kits market is segmented into two primary types: venous blood collection kits and capillary blood collection kits. Venous blood collection kits are designed for obtaining blood samples from veins, typically used in hospitals and laboratories for diagnostic testing. In contrast, capillary blood collection kits are used for obtaining blood from capillaries, often via fingerstick, making them ideal for point-of-care testing and at-home use. Each type serves distinct needs in medical diagnostics and patient care.</p></p>
