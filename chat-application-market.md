<p><p>Chat applications are categorized into two main market types: cloud-based and on-premise. Cloud-based chat applications offer scalability and flexibility, as they are hosted on the provider's servers and accessed via the internet. This model typically requires lower upfront costs and allows easy updates and maintenance.
