<p><p>The carbonless continuous paper market is divided into two main types: single-layer and multi-layer. Single-layer carbonless paper consists of a single sheet coated with microencapsulated dye, allowing for a single copy when pressure is applied. In contrast, multi-layer carbonless paper features multiple layers, enabling the production of multiple copies simultaneously, typically ranging from two to five layers. These variations cater to different printing needs across various industries, balancing efficiency and cost-effectiveness in documentation processes.</p></p>
