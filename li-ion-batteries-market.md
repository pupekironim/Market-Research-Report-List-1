<p><p>The Li-ion battery market comprises various types based on materials used for the cathode. Lithium Cobalt Oxide (LCO) offers high energy density, ideal for mobile devices. Lithium Iron Phosphate (LFP) is known for safety and thermal stability, suitable for electric vehicles. Lithium Nickel Cobalt Aluminum Oxide (NCA) provides high energy and power, used in EVs. Lithium Manganese Oxide (LMO) offers improved thermal stability. Lithium Titanate (LTO) excels in rapid charging and long life, while Lithium Nickel Manganese Cobalt (NMC) balances energy and power for diverse applications.</p></p>
