<p><p>The MOCVD susceptors market is primarily divided into CVD SiC coated graphite susceptors and CVI SiC coated graphite susceptors. CVD SiC coated susceptors are produced through chemical vapor deposition, offering excellent thermal and chemical stability for efficient MOCVD processes. In contrast, CVI SiC coated susceptors are made using chemical vapor infiltration, providing superior mechanical strength and uniformity. Both types play critical roles in semiconductor manufacturing, enhancing the performance of the MOCVD process by ensuring optimal substrate heating and material deposition.</p></p>
