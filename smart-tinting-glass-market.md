<p><p>The smart-tinting glass market comprises three main types: photochromic, electrochromic, and thermochromic. Photochromic glass automatically darkens in response to sunlight, providing dynamic shading. Electrochromic glass changes tint through electrical stimulation, allowing for adjustable light control and privacy. Thermochromic glass reacts to temperature changes, altering its opacity based on heat levels. Each type offers unique benefits, such as energy efficiency, enhanced comfort, and aesthetic appeal, catering to various applications in residential, commercial, and automotive sectors.</p></p>
