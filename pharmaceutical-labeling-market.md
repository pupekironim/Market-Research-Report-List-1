<p><strong>The Pharmaceutical Labeling Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pressure-Sensitive Labels</li><li>Glue-Applied Labels</li><li>Sleeve Labels</li><li>In-Mold Labels</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pharmaceutical labeling market encompasses various types of labels designed for product identification and compliance. Pressure-sensitive labels are self-adhesive and easily applied, making them versatile for various packaging. Glue-applied labels require an adhesive for application and are typically used on glass or plastic containers. Sleeve labels wrap around the entire container, providing a 360-degree display and tamper evidence. In-mold labels are integrated into the packaging during production, ensuring durability and resistance to environmental factors. Each type serves specific branding and regulatory needs.</p></p>
