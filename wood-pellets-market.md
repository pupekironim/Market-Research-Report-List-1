<p><strong>The Wood-Pellets Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>White Pellet</li><li>Black Pellet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wood pellet market is primarily divided into two types: white pellets and black pellets. White pellets are produced from clean wood biomass, typically used for residential heating and industrial applications, valued for their high energy content and low ash output. Conversely, black pellets are made from lignin-rich biomass and undergo a torrefaction process, enhancing durability and energy density. These are increasingly utilized in power generation, particularly as a coal substitute, due to their ability to improve combustion efficiency.</p></p>
