<p><strong>The Fan Impeller Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Closed Impeller</li><li>Semi-Open Impeller</li><li>Open Impeller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fan impeller market comprises three main types: closed, semi-open, and open impellers. Closed impellers feature two parallel plates, providing high efficiency and excellent pressure generation, making them suitable for high-performance applications. Semi-open impellers have a single plate with fewer restrictions, allowing for versatility and ease of maintenance, ideal for handling varying fluid characteristics. Open impellers consist of blades attached to a hub without casing, promoting easy passage of solid-laden fluids, suitable for applications where solid content is a concern.</p></p>
