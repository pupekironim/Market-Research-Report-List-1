<p><strong>The Offshore Mooring Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Drag Embedment Anchors (DEA)</li><li>Vertical Load Anchors (VLA)</li><li>Suction Anchors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The offshore mooring market features various anchor types, including Drag Embedment Anchors (DEA), Vertical Load Anchors (VLA), and Suction Anchors. DEAs are buried into the seabed, utilizing drag resistance for stability in dynamic environments. VLAs are designed to support vertical loads, ideal for deepwater applications. Suction Anchors rely on the difference in pressure created during installation to embed themselves effectively, providing substantial holding power. Each type caters to specific environmental conditions and operational requirements in offshore projects.</p></p>
