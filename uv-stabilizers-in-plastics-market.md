Major types include hindered amine light stabilizers (HALS) that prevent degradation by neutralizing free radicals, UV absorbers that absorb harmful UV light and dissipate it as heat, and quenchers that dissipate energy without causing damage. Other categories may include various specialized additives tailored to specific applications. These stabilizers ensure durability and aesthetic appeal in plastics used in outdoor environments.</p></p>
