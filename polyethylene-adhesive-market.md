<p><ul><li>Water-based Adhesive</li><li>Solvent-based Adhesive</li><li>Hot Melt Adhesive</li><li>Pressure Sensitive Adhesive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Polyethylene Adhesive market comprises several types: </p><p>1. **Water-based Adhesives** are eco-friendly, utilizing water as a solvent, making them suitable for various applications with low VOC emissions. </p><p>2. **Solvent-based Adhesives** contain organic solvents, providing strong bonding for tough materials but may release harmful fumes. </p><p>3. **Hot Melt Adhesives** are thermoplastic and offer quick setting times, ideal for rapid production processes. </p><p>4. **Pressure Sensitive Adhesives** create a bond upon application of light pressure, widely used in tapes and labels for easy adhesion and removal.</p></p>
