<p><p>The OP Wax market consists of two primary types: Crude Lignite Wax and Refined Lignite Wax. Crude Lignite Wax is derived from the initial extraction processes of lignite coal, retaining impurities and natural characteristics. In contrast, Refined Lignite Wax undergoes further purification, enhancing its properties for various applications, including cosmetics, candles, and coatings. The distinction between these types is crucial for industries seeking specific qualities like melting point, color, and odor, influencing their end-use applications.</p></p>
