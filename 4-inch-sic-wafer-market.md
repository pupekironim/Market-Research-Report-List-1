<p><p>The 4-inch SiC wafer market is categorized into two primary types: conductive SiC wafers and semi-insulating SiC wafers. Conductive SiC wafers, typically doped to create either n-type or p-type properties, are essential for high-power and high-frequency electronic devices. In contrast, semi-insulating SiC wafers possess minimal conductivity, making them ideal for high-frequency applications and reducing parasitic capacitance. Together, these wafer types facilitate advancements in power electronics and RF applications, catering to various industrial and technological needs.</p></p>
