<p><p>The stairlift market is primarily divided into two types: straight stairlifts and curved stairlifts. Straight stairlifts are designed for straightforward, linear staircases, offering a cost-effective solution for homes with simple configurations. In contrast, curved stairlifts are custom-built to navigate turns, landings, or unique staircase shapes, making them ideal for more complex installations. Both types improve accessibility for individuals with mobility challenges, enhancing independence and safety within multi-level homes. Their demand reflects the growing need for personalized mobility solutions.</p></p>
