<p><ul><li>Transmission</li><li>Reflection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical pulse and heart rate sensor market can be categorized into two main types: transmission and reflection sensors. Transmission sensors work by emitting light through a medium, such as a finger or earlobe, and measuring the light absorbed by blood vessels. In contrast, reflection sensors detect the light reflected off the skin's surface, allowing for heart rate monitoring without penetration. Both types offer unique advantages in various applications, from fitness tracking to medical diagnostics, catering to different user needs.</p></p>
