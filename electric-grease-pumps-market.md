<p><strong>The Electric Grease Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Self-priming</li><li>Submersible</li><li>Suction Head</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electric grease pumps are categorized into three main types based on their operational mechanisms: self-priming, submersible, and suction head pumps. Self-priming pumps can draw grease from lower levels automatically, making them ideal for various applications. Submersible pumps are designed to operate fully submerged in the grease, providing efficient transport. Suction head pumps rely on atmospheric pressure to draw grease from a low point, making them suitable for specific setups. Each type caters to varying industrial demands, enhancing lubrication processes effectively.</p></p>
