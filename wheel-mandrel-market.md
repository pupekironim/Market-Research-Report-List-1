<p><p>The wheel mandrel market comprises two primary types: Jet Wheel Mandrel and Hammer Wheel Mandrel. Jet Wheel Mandrels utilize high-speed air pressure for efficient material shaping and are ideal for precision work in industries like automotive and aerospace. In contrast, Hammer Wheel Mandrels rely on a mechanical hammering action to achieve the desired form, often used in heavy-duty applications such as metal forging. Both types serve unique manufacturing needs, enhancing productivity and quality in various sectors.</p></p>
