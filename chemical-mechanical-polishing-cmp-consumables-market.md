<p><p>The Chemical Mechanical Polishing (CMP) consumables market consists of three primary types: polishing liquids, polishing pads, and other materials. Polishing liquids, including slurries, facilitate material removal and surface smoothing by providing necessary chemicals and abrasive particles. Polishing pads serve as the substrate that contacts the wafer surface, aiding in material abrasion and achieving desired surface finishes. Other consumables might include cleaning agents and conditioning disks, which enhance the effectiveness of the polishing process and maintain equipment performance.</p></p>
