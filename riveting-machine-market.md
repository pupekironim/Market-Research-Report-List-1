<p><p>The riveting machine market includes several key types, each serving specific applications. Impact riveting utilizes high force impact for rapid joint assembly, while orbital riveting applies rotational motion for precision and low distortion. Radial riveting employs a spiral motion to ensure tight connections, and rollerform riveting uses rollers to create strong joints without heat. Automatic drilling and riveting machines streamline the process by integrating drilling and riveting tasks, enhancing efficiency and reducing labor costs in manufacturing environments.</p></p>
