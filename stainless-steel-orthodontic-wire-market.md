<p><p>The stainless steel orthodontic wire market is segmented into two main types: multi-strand stainless steel wire and single strand stainless steel wire. Multi-strand stainless steel wire consists of multiple strands twisted together, offering enhanced flexibility and strength, making it ideal for complex orthodontic treatments. In contrast, single strand stainless steel wire is rigid and provides excellent durability and stability, effective for simpler alignment procedures. Both types are essential for various orthodontic applications, catering to different treatment needs.</p></p>
